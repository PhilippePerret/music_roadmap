Signing Up
  • Click on Signin/Signup Button ;
    -> Sign In Form opens.
  • Click on Sign up Button ;
    -> Sign Up Form opens.
  • Fill every field (take care of the “Instrument” field, it'll let you choose exercices of your instrument from the database) ;
  • Then click on “Sign Up” button.
You will receave a mail confirmation. Keep it, it contains your email address and your password so you will be able to sign in next time to Music Roadmap.
