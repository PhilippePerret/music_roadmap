Exercices Database
The exercices database contains exercice data to add to your roadmap, defining all the required caracteristics (technic types, tempi, etc.).
To choose an exercice, you just have to open the database and to choose the exercice, which may be classified by author or by collection (when the collection contains several authors).
This database grows up day after day. Phil doesn't have any priority for the next exercices added, so feel free to submit to him the exercices you need.
