General Configuration of Exercices
The “General configuration of exercices” defines three parameters to vary the work on the exercices:
  • Exercice direction;
  • Harmonic suite;
  • Exercices order.
Saving the general configuration of exercices
To save your current configuration, click the config wheel to choose the required configuration, then click on the save button “Save configuration”.
It worths noting that when you ask for a preparing working session, a option checkboxd accomplish the same goal.
