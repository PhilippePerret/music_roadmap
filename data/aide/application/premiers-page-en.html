Quick Start
Welcome to “Music Roadmap”!
With Music Roadmap, you can:
  • Keep a list of your daily exercices;
  • Assist you to play them, in a certain or a random order, with the tempo you defined, by clicking a single button or a key;
  • Memorize your progress in a log to give you a real overview of your progress;
  • Organize for you the work session, according to your schedule time, the exercices you've already worked or/and the difficulties you want to work on.
To see a sample — and learn to load a music roadmap —, visit the “Music Roadmap Sample” page.
