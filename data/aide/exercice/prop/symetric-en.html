Symetric Exercice
An exercice is “symetric” when you can play it in ascending order or in descending order. It's the case for example with most of the “Hanon” exercices.
When Music Roadmap prepares a session, it deals with this property to provide a different direction during the work session.
If “Symetric” checkbox is checked in the exercice form, the exercice is a symetric exercice.
