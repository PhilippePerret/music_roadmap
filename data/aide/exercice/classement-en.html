Sorting exercices
Prerequise
To be able to sort your exercices, you must be signed up (Sign Up) and signed in on Music Roadmap (Sign In) and you must have a musical roadmap opened (Create a Roadmap, Load a Roadmap).
  • The exercice list is showed?… So click on Moving button (“Activate moving”) ;
  • Sort your exercices, simply dragging them with the mouse ;
  • Click on End Moving Button (“Stop Moving”) to record the new exercice list.
